Painting on Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh meaning ‘in the lap of the Himalayas’ or ‘land of snowy mountains’ is also referred to as Nature’s Paradise or Dev Bhumi. The culture in Himachal Pradesh is a rich mix of Hindus, tribes like Kinnars, Gaddis, Gujjars, Buddhists, Christians and Muslims. As it is bordered by Jammu and Kashmir, Punjab, Haryana, Uttarakhand and Tibet, the culture of Himachal Pradesh is extremely varied and deeply influenced by all these cultures.
